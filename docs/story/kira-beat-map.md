# Kira Nightwind — Beat Map

## Arc overview

Kira starts CH1 wanting simple things: to survive the night her home burned and to find out what happened to her parents. Pursuing that costs her innocence in pieces — each use of void magic, each bargain, each kill chips at the girl she was, and by CH2-3 "find my parents" has curdled into "find the crown" and "find my mother," entangling her with the Cult and the Hollow King's legacy she never asked for. By CH4 the want has changed shape entirely: it is no longer about the people she lost but about what she is becoming, forcing a reckoning with the void inside her (embrace it, suppress it, or balance it) before she can face anything else. CH5 collapses all of that into one final choice — claim the crown, destroy it, walk away, or become something beyond either — and the ending she reaches is the answer to who she decided to be, not what she set out to do.

## Chapter beats (CH1 through CH5)

### CH1
- **Want**: Survive, and find out what happened to her parents / the night her home burned.
- **Cost**: Her innocence — first use of void magic, first kill, first lie to someone who trusted her.
- **Closing note**: Grief hardening into resolve; she leaves with a crown piece and no home to return to.
- **Climax scenes**: KIRA_CH1_CROWN_FIRST, KIRA_CH1_TRUTH, KIRA_CH1_PARENTS, KIRA_CH1_BANDIT_AMBUSH

### CH2
- **Want**: Reach Crownhaven, get closer to the crown, and find her mother.
- **Cost**: Trust — allies compromised, deeper and more overt use of void magic, direct exposure to the Cult.
- **Closing note**: The crown's pull deepens as her mother's fate turns out to be tangled with it; there is no clean version of "finding her" left.
- **Climax scenes**: KIRA_CH2_CROWNHAVEN, KIRA_CH2_BATTLE, KIRA_CH2_CROWN, KIRA_CH2_MOTHER

### CH3
- **Want**: Gain the power and allies she needs to stand against the Hollow King.
- **Cost**: A bargain that takes something intimate and irreversible — not just strength, but a piece of who she was willing to be.
- **Closing note**: She has power now and barely recognizes herself; the price is already paid, there's no undoing it.
- **Climax scenes**: KIRA_CH3_TEMPLE, KIRA_CH3_TALK, KIRA_CH3_BARGAIN, KIRA_CH3_CHARGE

### CH4
- **Want**: Decide, before the end, what she is going to become — weapon, vessel, or something she can still live with.
- **Cost**: Certainty. Every path (embrace, suppress, balance) costs a different piece of her — control, connection, or peace.
- **Closing note**: She walks into the final chapter already changed, and there's no version of the last battle that isn't shaped by that choice.
- **Climax scenes**: KIRA_CH4_MAIN, KIRA_CH4_REALITY_CHECK, KIRA_CH4_POWER, KIRA_CH4_SUPPRESS

### CH5
- **Want**: End it — claim or destroy the crown, end the Hollow King, and settle, once and for all, what she is.
- **Cost**: Whatever she has left. This is the choice with no partial refund.
- **Closing note**: The moment tips irreversibly into one of the ending families — whoever she becomes, the crown and the void both get their answer.
- **Climax scenes**: KIRA_CH5_MAIN, KIRA_CH5_CONFRONT, KIRA_CH5_CROWN, KIRA_CH5_BATTLE

## Ending families

**crown-claimed** — She takes the crown and its power for herself, for better or worse.
Members: KIRA_ENDING_BALANCE, KIRA_ENDING_EMPIRE, KIRA_ENDING_MASTER, KIRA_ENDING_UNDYING
Emotional truth: *Power answered her call, and she said yes without flinching.*

**mother** — The ending turns on what happened to her mother and what that costs or confirms.
Members: KIRA_ENDING_HOLLOW, KIRA_ENDING_SURVIVOR, KIRA_ENDING_TRUTH, KIRA_ENDING_VICTOR
Emotional truth: *Whatever she found at the end, it led back to her mother's face.*

**walked-away** — She refuses the crown and the fight both, choosing an ordinary, unresolved life.
Members: KIRA_ENDING, KIRA_ENDING_FREE
Emotional truth: *Walking away wasn't defeat — it was the first choice that was entirely hers.*

**consumed-by-void** — The void wins, fully or partially; she becomes something no longer just human.
Members: KIRA_ENDING_BRIDGE, KIRA_ENDING_GODLIKE, KIRA_ENDING_GUARDIAN, KIRA_ENDING_LOST
Emotional truth: *She reached for the power so many times that it stopped being a tool and became her.*

**burden-kept** — She survives by choosing to carry the cost for others rather than for herself.
Members: KIRA_ENDING_DEFENDER, KIRA_ENDING_HEALER, KIRA_ENDING_HERO, KIRA_ENDING_MARTYR, KIRA_ENDING_MENTOR, KIRA_ENDING_ORDER, KIRA_ENDING_PROTECTOR, KIRA_ENDING_PURIFIER, KIRA_ENDING_SACRIFICE, KIRA_ENDING_WARRIOR
Emotional truth: *She stopped asking what the crown would give her and started asking what she owed everyone else.*

## Payoff threads

1. **Mother's voice** (style-bible anchor). Seed at KIRA_CH1_PARENTS (the last real memory of her, half-formed, before the fire). Deepen at KIRA_CH2_MOTHER (Crownhaven — the voice becomes a person again, or a ghost of one). Pay off at KIRA_CH5_REFLECT / KIRA_CH5_MAIN, where the description already turns explicitly to "mother... father... the cottage burned girl ran from it" in the quiet before the final choice — this is the natural landing point for the half-remembered voice to resolve.

2. **Silver crescent of the Circle** (style-bible anchor). Seed at KIRA_CH1_CIRCLE_SERVICE / KIRA_CH1_OFFER (the Circle's first approach and its mark). Carry through KIRA_CH3_CIRCLE. Pay off at KIRA_CH4_CIRCLE (Magistra Venn, the alliance tested) and resolve at KIRA_CH5_ALLIES, where the ORDER/DEFENDER/MENTOR branch decides what the crescent ultimately means to her.

3. **Frostbite scars** (style-bible anchor). Seed at KIRA_CH1_CROWN_FIRST (her first real use of void magic — the cost cue the style bible requires). Pay off at KIRA_CH4_REALITY_CHECK, where she has to look at her own hands and reckon with what using the void has already done to her, and again at KIRA_CH5_LIMIT if that branch is reached (scars as proof of how far she's pushed past the edge).

4. **Tom, the orphan boy**. Seed at KIRA_CH1_ORPHAN (the choice to help him or leave him to his fate). Pay off at KIRA_CH2_COMPANION, the scene the data confirms he leads directly into — whether he becomes an ally she protects or a person she failed says something about which Kira the player is building.

5. **The crown itself, as Nightwind inheritance**. Seed at KIRA_CH1_CROWN_FIRST (first piece found, first taste of the hunger). Pay off at KIRA_CH5_CROWN, where the pieces "vibrate in harmony" and the prose already names that a Nightwind wore it before her — the seed should plant that ancestral hunger early so this payoff lands as recognition, not a new idea.
